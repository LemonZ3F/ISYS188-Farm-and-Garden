About US


We here at Big Rapids Farm and Garden value our customers with the utmost respect. We want you to have the best for your animals, and gardens.
History
Big Rapids Farm and Garden was founded in the fall of 1968. In 1980, the first grain dryer bins were constructed. Five years later a scale and warehouse were added to hold bulk fertilizer and weigh bulk grain, lime, and fertilizer. Then in 1998, a brand new storefront was constructed.
Big Rapids Farm and Garden is known for having a knowledgeable staff and exceptional service. Today, we strive to hold our old time commitment of providing feed and supplies to a broad range of customers in Mecosta County and the surrounding areas of: Montcalm, Isabella, Osceola, Newaygo and Wexford counties. We are dedicated to supporting these areas and are excited to share our animal and farm knowledge with you.

The staff of Big Rapids Farm & Garden are available to help answer any questions you have. Please contact us by calling our store Monday through Friday 8am – 5pm and Saturday 8am – 3pm.
We offer deliverary of goods, and rentals so you can reseieve your purchases. Here at Big Rapids Farn and Garden we have farm chemicals, fertilizer, seeds. We also offer lawn and garden seeds, and fertilizer. Lastly we offer food, and other supplies for your animals, and if we don't have what you are looking for we can make a sepcial order for you.
